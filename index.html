Feeling Anxious?

we're here to help ~

This experience is designed to help you calm your anxious mind using breathing techniques

Give yourself some time to unwind

Headphones are highly recommended

Start your journey here

Step One.
Accept the Anxiety.

The natural instinct when you're feeling anxious,
Is to reject it and want to run away from it,
To ignore it and hope that it'll go away,
But often, trying so hard to ignore it makes us feel worse,

Instead, a better approach is accept that the feeling will pass,
Know that you are anxious,
Know that you're going to feel a little unsettled,
That you're going to be uncomfortable for a while,

Know that despite it all,
You'll be okay.
Breathe.
Breathe Deeply,

Letting your chest and stomach expand as you breathe in,
Filling your lungs up to the fullest extent,

Before you breathe out slowly,

Here's a little help~
Inhale
Sustain
Exhale
Relax
Be Mindful and Present.

Gently guide your mind to focus on the present,

Focus on the sounds and scenery around you,
The colors, the contrasts
The motion and movement

If the environment around you is a bit too hectic,
You could try picking one of the handcrafted scenes,
Designed and developed,
For the sole purpose of anxiety relief.
